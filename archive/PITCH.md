# Project Pitch

## The Problem

Building generalist physical AI requires millions of high-quality, linguistically rich robotic trajectories, but manual data collection and semantic labeling remain the costliest and slowest bottlenecks in the robotics industry.

---

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

**Enterprise Robotics Labs & OEMs** — Companies building or deploying specialized robotic arms (e.g., logistics, lab automation, advanced manufacturing) that need to rapidly adapt lightweight edge models to bespoke industrial environments and workflows.

**Physical AI Research Teams** — Teams utilizing open-source frameworks (like Hugging Face's LeRobot) who are bottlenecked by small, monotonous imitation-learning datasets that fail to generalize.

---

## The Solution

A hardware-agnostic, synthetic data-generation engine that scales robotics datasets 10x without extra manual labor. By capturing minimal raw physical trajectories and pairing them with a multi-modal agent (powered by Claude), the pipeline automatically generates diverse semantic variations, dense visual annotations, and edge-case perturbations — producing optimized, deployment-ready LeRobot datasets designed to tune edge models like SmolVLA locally in minutes.

---

## One-Liner

**VLA-DataEngine** accelerates the deployment of physical AI by using a generative dual-model pipeline to synthesize high-fidelity, semantically rich training data for edge robotics, slashing development timelines from months to hours.
